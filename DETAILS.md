# Wasteland Zero

**Wasteland Zero** is a fast 2D top-down shooter made with React and TypeScript. Jump into short, chaotic runs, scavenge weapons and parts, and mix and match attachments for wild, arcade-style combat. The game is easy to pick up and focused on replayable sessions and fun weapon combinations.

## Overview
Wasteland Zero drops you into a ruined world where every fight is short and intense. You explore small maps, grab weapons and gear, and face waves of enemies that force you to adapt. Runs are designed to be replayable and experimental, so you can try different builds and strategies without a big time commitment.

## Story
You play as a scavenger surviving after society broke down. There is no long campaign. Story elements appear as scraps, like graffiti, logs, and item descriptions. The focus is on atmosphere and small moments rather than deep narrative.

## Core loop
- Spawn into a compact area
- Scavenge weapons, ammo, and parts
- Fight enemies, collect loot and upgrades
- Tweak your loadout and try another run

## Features
- **Short, replayable runs** that suit casual play sessions
- **Modular weapons**: attach barrels, ammo types, and mods to change how guns behave
- **Scavenge and craft**: find parts during runs to tweak gear on the fly
- **Varied enemies** with different behaviors and threats
- **Simple controls** with depth from weapon synergies and attachments
- **Small persistent progression**: unlock new parts, cosmetics, and minor upgrades

## Gameplay mechanics
- Twin stick style aiming and movement (keyboard and gamepad supported)
- Weapon parts change damage, spread, fire rate, and special effects
- Some weapons use limited ammo, others recharge or overheat
- Short term run modifiers change loot, enemy spawns, or player stats for variety
- Health is limited but forgiving; risky plays can yield bigger rewards
- Enemy difficulty scales within a run to keep tension rising

## Visuals and sound
- Readable, gritty art style that prioritizes clarity in hectic combat
- Impactful sound design with punchy hits and satisfying feedback
- Minimal HUD focused on health, ammo, and current run modifiers
