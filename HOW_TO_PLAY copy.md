# How to Play

## Quick Start
1. Start a run and spawn into the map.
2. Scavenge nearby weapons, ammo, and parts.
3. Engage enemies, collect loot, and upgrade gear between encounters.
4. When you fall, try a new loadout and keep experimenting.

## Instructions
- Move around to avoid enemy fire and reposition for better shots.
- Aim independently from movement to dodge while keeping pressure on enemies.
- Collect parts and attachments to change how weapons behave.
- Use consumables like medkits when low on health.

## Controls
**Keyboard & mouse**
- Move: `W`, `A`, `S`, `D`
- Aim: Mouse
- Fire: Left click
- Alternate fire / Use: Right click
- Dash / Roll: `Space`

- Play aggressively early to collect parts quickly.
- If a weapon runs out of ammo, switch to a backup or scavenge more.
- Learn enemy patterns in short runs to plan effective loadouts.

## Troubleshooting
- If you feel stuck, try a different combination of attachments or a more defensive playstyle.
- Use consumables sparingly but smartly; medkits can save runs.
